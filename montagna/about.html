Welcome to Montagna

We created our dining establishment in 2022 with a desire to produce a veritable experience. We aim to please those of you who want a well-orchestrated dining experience without any unnecessarily expensive ornamentation.

Our founder, Arduino once owned one of the largest mountain ranges in all of Italy. He would create beautiful Italian dishes from his balcony. Upon moving to America, Arduino imagined this restaurant to never forget his culture. Ever since, we have been devoted to putting authentic quality into your dining while maintaining a causal atmosphere.
